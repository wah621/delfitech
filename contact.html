Common Questions

What's your typical lead time for manufacturing?

Lead times vary based on product complexity and volume. Standard projects typically range from 4-8 weeks. Contact us for specific timelines.

Do you handle low-volume production?

Yes, we serve both high-volume and low-volume manufacturing needs. We can discuss minimum order quantities based on your project requirements.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible payment terms tailored to your needs. Standard terms are typically 30 days net from invoice, with options for deposits and milestone payments.

Can you handle custom design and development?

Absolutely. We offer complete product design and development services. Our engineering team can work with you from concept through production.

What about quality assurance and testing?

We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and perform comprehensive testing including functional testing, hi-pot testing, and environmental validation.

Do you offer logistics and distribution?

Yes, through our Denmark logistics hub, we provide European distribution, warehousing, and fulfillment services for our manufacturing partners.
