About Delfi Tech Manufacturing

Three decades of excellence in global manufacturing

Our Journey Since 1992

1992

Founded in Hong Kong

Delfi Tech Manufacturing Ltd. (DTM) established as a precision electronics manufacturing company, serving the Asia-Pacific region.

1996

Factory in Shenzhen

Expanded manufacturing capacity with a state-of-the-art facility in Shenzhen, China (DTC), positioning for high-volume production.

2002

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved international quality standards across all operations. First certification in 2002, continuously maintained and upgraded.

2010

European Expansion

Established sales office in Germany (Delfi Tech Deutschland) to serve European markets with localized support and faster delivery.

2013

Hungarian Production

Opened production facility in Hungary (Delfi Hungary) to provide strategic proximity to Central European customers.

2018

Logistics Hub in Denmark

Opened logistics support center in Denmark (Delfi Denmark) for optimized European distribution and inventory management.

2020

Factory Relocation

Relocated Shenzhen facility to modern infrastructure for expanded capacity, advanced automation, and enhanced environmental standards.

2024

Global Industry Leader

Serving 13+ major customers across Europe and Asia. ISO 9001:2015 certified. Positioned as trusted EMS & OEM partner worldwide.
